Dropzio

Dropzio is a geolocation app for android and iOS mobile devices. Dopzio hasn’t found it’s exact voice yet, but the idea is allowing our users to create organic content for other users. Users create drops with images and content from their exact location The posts then become visible to other users within a 60ft vicinity. Users view single posts and vote on them. Users may also use the compass feature to discover posts outside of their discovery vicinity. Some potential applications: College Orientation, Tourist tours of cities, Pub Crawls, Scavenger hunts, Business advertising/marketing or just for fun. Keep posted this app will be available on the apple and google play store.
